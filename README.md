Nkanyezi Learning Management System (LMS)
A Work-Integrated Learning Project | Digitizing Facilitator Operations

Overview
Nkanyezi LMS is a comprehensive digital solution built to transform facilitator operations in training environments. Developed during a work-integrated learning placement, this system addresses critical inefficiencies in traditional paper-based training management.

The Problem: The training department was spending approximately R5000 per day on paper reams alone, with additional costs from lost registers, manual tracking errors, and administrative overhead.

The Solution: A three-pronged digital approach that eliminates paper waste, provides real-time insights, and automates routine facilitator tasks.

Key Features
1. GPS-Based Attendance Tracking

Location-verified check-ins using learner device GPS
Compares learner location against organization GPS coordinates
50-meter proximity threshold for valid attendance capture
100% elimination of lost or damaged paper registers
Real-time identification of at-risk learners based on attendance patterns

2. Real-Time Facilitator Dashboard

Instantaneous monitoring of learner attendance
Live generation of learner progress reports
No more carrying stacked files between classrooms
Immediate visibility of participation trends and anomalies

3. Automated Feedback System

Script-driven instant feedback generation
Based on combined learner progress and attendance data
Scales seamlessly from small classes to large cohorts
Reduces facilitator administrative burden significantly

5. SAQA-Aligned Rollout Plan Creator
   
Replace complex Excel workflows with intuitive interface
Generate compliant rollout plans in minutes, not hours
Fine-tune plans on the fly
Ensures regulatory alignment without manual verification

6. Interactive Qualifications Management
   
Dynamic qualification browsing and tracking
Visual progress mapping for learners
Simplified compliance reporting
